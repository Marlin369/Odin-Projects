Balushai

Balushai is a traditional Indian sweet, known for its crispy texture and rich flavor. It is made from flour, sugar, and yogurt, and is often flavored with cardamom or saffron.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, yogurt, ghee, and cardamom powder. Mix well to form a dough.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them slightly.
  5. Fry the flattened dough in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
