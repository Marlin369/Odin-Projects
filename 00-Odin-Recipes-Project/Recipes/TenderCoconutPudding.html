Tender Coconut Pudding Recipe

Tender Coconut Pudding is a delightful dessert made with fresh coconut water, tender coconut meat, and a few simple ingredients. It's a light and refreshing treat, perfect for warm weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tender coconut water
  • 1 cup tender coconut meat, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the tender coconut water and sugar. Heat gently until the sugar dissolves.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the cornstarch and water to form a slurry.
  3. Slowly add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped tender coconut meat and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the mixture into serving cups and refrigerate until set.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves before serving.
